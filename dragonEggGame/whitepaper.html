The Dragon Egg Game

In an ancient city called Aurora valley, the dragons were living peacefully and everything was good. Till… one day, the dragons found out that the queen's egg was missing.

Everyone was scared, what happened? Did someone steal the rarest egg in the valley? Three dragons quickly formed a team and started searching for every egg in the whole world, flying over villages and cities hoping to find the right one.

Who will win the big bounty?

---------------

In this game you will find two main characters: dragons and eggs.

 There will be three types of dragons: commun, EPIC and RARE. There will be different eggs that will be producing more YOLK overtime but the most important one will be the missing egg.
This very 1/1 unique egg will be producing 10x more YOLK than the other random eggs it will be given to the biggest Eggs NFT holder.
They will also be a unique dragon (it will be the Missing dragon) this dragon will be given to you if you’re the top holder of YOLK 5 days after game launch this dragon give you +100 token each day.

All the dragons have one goal: search for the queen’s egg.

Through this journey they will be capturing different eggs from different villages that will produce them more YOLK. They can also capture other dragons to force them to search eggs with them.
